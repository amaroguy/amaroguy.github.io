WHAT IS TRITON BARBELL?

Triton Barbell is a powerlifting and weightlifting community that aims to promote knowledge and competition in strength sports.

Our goal is to create a friendly, welcoming environment in which everyone, regardless of personal goals or experience level, can pursue self-empowerment through lifting.

WHAT DO WE DO?

BUILD COMMUNITY

We place heavy emphasis on community at Triton Barbell.
We do events on a weekly and quarterly basis such as:

Weekly
Community Workouts:
Every saturday at 11 A.M., members line up their schedules to all come to RIMAC at the same time. This is the perfect place to come ask questions and meet new gym friends (besides from our Discord ).
Sometimes they are themed so that you can easily find TBB members to talk to! The image under "ACHIEVE GOALS" was taken at our halloween themed gathering, where we encouraged people to come in costume.

Quarterly
Mock Powerlifting Meets:
Hosted around week 8 of each quarter, Tritons gain experience in competing in a powerlifting competition, and have the chance to max out in Squat, Bench, and Deadlift in a competitive environment.

Quarterly
Brunch:
Also hosted near the end of the quarter, we go to a park and celebrate a successfull quarter, and make announcements about upcoming events.

ACHIEVE GOALS

Triton Barbell is a place to ask questions and learn from one another.

Whether you are doing CrossFit, Bodybuilding, Powerlifting, or Calisthenics, Triton Barbell is meant to be a place to learn about fitness.
We have helped plenty of people learn about fitness, and there are fitness veterans as community members that would be happy to help you achieve your goals too.

You can join the community now by joining our Discord server, where you can keep up to date on events, meet new people, and ask questions.
