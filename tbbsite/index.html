What is Triton Barbell?

Triton Barbell is a powerlifting and weightlifting community that aims to promote knowledge and competition in strength sports.

We create a friendly, welcoming environment in which everyone, regardless of personal goals or experience level, can pursue self-empowerment through lifting.

What do we do?

Achieve Goals

Triton Barbell is a place to ask questions and learn from one another.

Whether its CrossFit, Bodybuilding, Powerlifting, or Calisthenics, Triton Barbell is meant to be a place to learn about fitness. We have fitness veterans that would be happy to help you achieve your goals.

Build Community

We place heavy emphasis on community at Triton Barbell.

Our discord is always open for advice, to find someone to go to the gym with, or maybe you just a cool place to hang out.

We also do quarterly and weekly events to get people together.
